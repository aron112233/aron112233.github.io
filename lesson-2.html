Lesson 2: Navigating the 3D Viewport

Objective: Learn how to orbit, pan, and zoom your view.

1. Quick Steps

  1. Middle-click + drag to **rotate** around your scene.
  2. Scroll wheel to **zoom** in and out.
  3. Shift + middle-click + drag to **pan** the view.
